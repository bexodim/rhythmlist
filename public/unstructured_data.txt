Soli 
names: suku, donba
locations: mali, senegal, guinea, mandingo
ethnic groups: malinke
notes: suku in Mali, Donba in Senegal, Mali (Malinke), guinea, Senegal; throughout Mandingo - Mali, guinea, Senegal; donba means big dance; Different  than furasoli/solifura
